About Student Academic Performance

The scholastic accomplishment of students in the Polytechnic is the most significant criterion to determine the nature of Polytechnic students. Studying the academic achievements of students is therefore of great importance for fostering student improvements and enhancing the standard of higher education . However, this accomplishment is influenced by existing student result . Usually, most higher institutions use the existing student result record of undergraduate students . While this is okay, the existing result is still influence to a degree . The quality of existing student result has a big influence on the next results of academic performance, research, and training. The principal goal of the admission framework is to decide the students who might perform well in the wake of existing results of the student in predicting of the next result. Hence, it is essential to analyse the academic potential of students.
